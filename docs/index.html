How it works

From job listing to finished documents
in three steps

1

Browse any job posting

Navigate to a listing on LinkedIn, a company career page, or any job board. Bespoke automatically extracts the job title, company, and description.

2

Right-click → Generate CV

Select text or right-click anywhere on the page and choose Generate CV for this job. Confirm the job details in the popup dialog.

3

Download your documents

Bespoke generates a tailored resume and cover letter, scores your profile match, and lets you download both as Markdown or PDF instantly.

4

Track your applications

Save the application and track its progress through every stage — from Applied to Offer — with notes, tags, and interview prep guides.

Features

Everything you need for a smarter job search

From document generation to application tracking, Bespoke covers the full job-application workflow.

Tailored Resume & Cover Letter

Both documents are generated in parallel. The resume highlights the skills and experience most relevant to the specific job description.

Smart Job Scraping

Extracts structured job data from LinkedIn via JSON-LD and CSS selectors. On other sites, an LLM parses the raw page text to pull out company, title, and description.

Profile Match Score

Get an instant 0–100% compatibility score with a breakdown of strengths, skill gaps, and concrete improvement suggestions for every application.

Application Tracker

Save every application and move it through the hiring pipeline: Saved → Applied → HR Interview → Technical Interviews → Offer / Reject.

Interview Preparation

Generate a focused technical interview prep guide for any saved role. Covers key technologies, likely questions, coding challenges, and deep-dive topics.

Analytics Dashboard

Visualise your job-search activity over time — applications by status, conversion rates, and trends to help you focus your effort.

PDF Export

Download your generated resume and cover letter as PDF directly from the dialog, ready to attach to any application.

Google Drive Sync

Your profile, settings, and applications automatically back up to your Google Drive's private app folder — seamlessly available across devices.

Fine-tune the output

Full control over how the AI writes

Six parameters let you dial in exactly the tone, focus, and scoring behaviour you want.

Temperature
Controls creativity and variation in the output.
default 0.7
Top-P
Nucleus sampling — lower values keep vocabulary conservative.
default 0.9
Max Tokens
Maximum length of each generated document.
default 4096
Writing Tone
Formal, Professional, or Conversational — injected into every prompt.
professional
Resume Focus
Lead with Skills, Experience, or a balanced layout.
balanced
Match Strictness
How rigorously skill gaps are penalised in the match score.
balanced

Your data stays with you

Your profile is stored locally in the browser. It is only sent to the AI endpoint you configure — never to any third-party service.

No tracking, no analytics

Bespoke contains no tracking pixels, no telemetry, and no usage analytics. What you do with it is entirely your business.
